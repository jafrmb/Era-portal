📜 Student & Guardian Agreement — El-Buhaj Royal Academy

I / We, the undersigned student and guardian, hereby agree to the following terms and conditions of El-Buhaj Royal Academy, Kano:


1. 📚 Conduct: The student will uphold the academy's code of conduct — Discipline, Knowledge & Leadership — and respect all teachers, peers, and school property at all times.


2. ⏰ Attendance: The student is expected to attend all scheduled classes punctually. Absences must be communicated in advance with valid reasons signed by the guardian.


3. 💳 Fees: School fees must be paid on time as per the academic calendar. Failure to pay may result in suspension of academic services.


4. 👔 Uniforms: Students must wear the official El-Buhaj Royal Academy uniform on all school days and designated events.


5. ⚠️ Discipline: Any act of bullying, violence, examination malpractice, or misconduct will be handled in accordance with the school's disciplinary policy, which may include suspension or expulsion.


6. 📱 Technology: Unauthorized use of mobile devices during school hours is strictly prohibited.


7. 📸 Media: The academy reserves the right to use student photos and academic achievements for school promotional materials unless otherwise notified in writing.


8. 🔒 Privacy: All personal information provided during registration will be kept confidential and used solely for academic administration.
